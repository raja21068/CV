About Me

Dr. Rajesh Kumar is an outstanding graduate of the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. He won the academic performance award and was recognized as an outstanding graduate student from 2016 to 2019. He collaborated on two national projects with Prof. Xiaosong Zhang during 2017-2019, including the 2019 National Natural Science Foundation Fund project titled "Research on Intelligent Identification Method of IoT Malware Based on Android." Additionally, he spearheaded the project "Blockchain-Based Secure Data Sharing" funded by the University of Electronic Science and Technology Talent Special Fund in 2019. Dr. Kumar has authored over 35 international journal and conference papers in the fields of Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Information Security, and has secured multiple government and university-level projects in 2019 and 2020.
